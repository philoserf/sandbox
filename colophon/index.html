Colophon

2021-09-29 | 98 words

The views expressed is built by Hugo, hosted on GitHub Pages, and cached by Cloudflare. It is developed on an Apple MacBook using VSCode, Hyper, and Zsh.

Content is written with a variety of editing tools. Text usually starts in Drafts on an iPhone or an iPad, and sometimes, even on an Apple Watch. Some ideas gain flesh and substance with mind-mapping in MindNode. Content that takes time to develop is tracked with Things.

Layout and presentation are prepared with HTML, CSS, and avoids JavaScript. When content is nearly ready to publish, it is marked up in Markdown.
